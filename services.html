What I check

Areas I inspect for a complete evaluation

Structural Integrity

The bones of the home — what holds everything up.

  • Foundation stability and signs of settlement
  • Walls, ceilings, and floors for cracks or damage
  • Basement, crawlspace, and beams for water intrusion or rot
  • Roof structure, trusses, and attic framing

Exterior Systems

The property's exterior is inspected for wear, damage, and potential issues.

  • Roofing materials, flashing, and gutters for leaks or damage
  • Siding, brickwork, and exterior finishes
  • Doors, windows, and seals for drafts and functionality
  • Drainage, grading, and walkways to prevent water intrusion

Interior Systems

Inside your property, I examine all major systems and living spaces for functionality and safety.

  • Walls, ceilings, and floors for damage or moisture signs
  • Windows and doors for alignment and seals
  • Staircases and railings for safety compliance
  • Kitchen appliances, cabinets, and fixtures

Mechanical & Safety Systems

I evaluate the key systems that keep your property running smoothly.

  • HVAC: heating, cooling, and ventilation systems for efficiency
  • Electrical: outlets, wiring, breaker panels, and grounding
  • Plumbing: pipes, fixtures, water heaters, and drainage
  • Safety: fireplaces, smoke detectors, carbon monoxide alarms

Specialized Areas

Certain aspects require extra attention to protect your investment.

  • Attic insulation and ventilation for energy efficiency
  • Garage and outbuildings for structural soundness
  • Decks, balconies, and porches for stability and safety
  • Visible pest damage or signs of wood-destroying organisms

Detailed Reporting

Every inspection ends with a clear, photo-backed report.

  • Photos and practical recommendations for any issues
