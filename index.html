About

Meet your inspector, Daniel Cullen

InspectorDan LLC is your trusted partner for real estate inspections. As a Certified Professional Inspector (CPI®) trained by InterNACHI, my work covers every major system — foundations, roofing, plumbing, electrical, and HVAC — so nothing gets overlooked.
